Our Story

Foraged Flora is a small cutting garden in the South Wairarapa, north east of the small township of Featherston.

The philosophy of the business is to provide affordable, sustainably grown , seasonal flowers to the local community.

All flowers and foliage grown on our property are organically grown and we work with other local flower farmers to bring you flowers throughout the year.

Our posies and bunches are available for sale at Adamsons Service Station in Featherston. If you are looking for special occasion or memorial flowers please email.
